ME 1011 Practice of Mechanical Engineering
Formula Air -- Propeller-Powered Racing Vehicles

Objective:
Design, test, analyze, and build a vehicle with the required functions:
- Autonomous (self-driven)
- Driven forward by the air induced by the rotating propeller(s) only
- Able to trace the black lines on the floor, cross bumpers and dodge barriers (track map as shown in the top right figure)
- Follow another vehicle

In this 5 people group project, my job was basically everything related to the fluids, including the design (Solidworks), numerical analysis (ANSYS Fluent), manufacture (3D printed), thrust test (wind tunnel experiment) of the propeller, the simulations, and drag coefficient measurements of the whole vehicle.

Tools Used

SolidWorks
3D printing (MEX)
ANSYS Fluent
Arduino
